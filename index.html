Aya Zirikly

Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science, George Washington University

Adjunct Assistant Professor, Center for Language and Speech Processing (CLSP), Johns Hopkins University

My research sits at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and healthcare. I focus on building computational systems that can understand clinical and social-media language to address critical challenges in mental health — including crisis detection, risk assessment, and depression identification. I also work on AI alignment and debiasing to ensure that NLP systems are fair, trustworthy, and equitable across communities.
